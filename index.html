Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Undergraduate Thesis

Proposed a biological network module-based zero-augmented generalized linear model, for differential expression analysis of count data. Conducted two simulation studies to compare the efficacy between zero-inflated model, hurdle model and negative binomial model. Then applied these to CPTAC colorectal cancer data set, and found ‘negative binomial hurdle model’ possesses higher efficacy and could identify modules with lower proportion size and effect size
